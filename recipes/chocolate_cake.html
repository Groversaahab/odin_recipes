Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tbsp Dutch processed coco powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla essence
  • 2 large eggs
  • 6 oz semi-sweet chocolate
  • 3/4 cup wipped cream

Steps to follow

  1. Preheat oven to 350 C
  2. Prepare pan by spraying oil/butter
  3. Whisk dry ingredients
  4. Mix in wet ingredients
  5. Pour batter into the prepped pan
  6. Bake for 27-35 min. Till centre is cooked
  7. Let it cool on a wire rack
