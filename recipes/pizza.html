Pizza

Sometimes all you want at the end of the day is a cheese pizza all to yourself. This recipe turns the simple into the sublime with the addition of an exceptional pizza dough and an easy-to-make tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • Pizza sauce:
    • 1 can tomato sauce
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 medium garlic cloves, grated
    • 1 1/2 tsp oregano
    • 3/4 tsp salt
    • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Pizza base:
    • 1 ball all purpose flour dough
    • All purpose flour
    • Semolina flour
    • 1/4 cup pizza sauce
    • 3 oz shredded mozzarella

Steps to follow

  1. Make a souce using pizza sauce ingredients
  2. Make a pizza base using the dough. Leave a border at the edges.
  3. Let the baking steel preheat at 260 degrees for 30 min
  4. Add sauce and topings on the pizza and bake for 3 minutes at 260 degrees
  5. Slice and serve hot
